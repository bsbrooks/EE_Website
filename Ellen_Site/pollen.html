Pollen

Pollen are particles that are released from trees, weeds and grasses during the spring, summer and fall. Their purpose is the fertilization of other plants. People who are allergic and have contact with pollen will exhibit symptoms such as:

Sneezing
Itchy eyes nose and throat
Watery eyes
Conjunctivitis
Wheezing
Coughing
Difficulty breathing

Contact Dr. Epstein to determine the cause of your pollen allergy. Treatment can be initiated immediately to relieve your symptoms.
